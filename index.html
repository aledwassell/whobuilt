What we do
Rigorous. Independent. Buyer-first.

Build quality scores

Aggregated ratings across structural defects, finishing, and snagging — verified against NHBC warranty data.

Verified resident reviews

Post-completion reviews from real homeowners — not purchase-day surveys filled in before the problems appear.

After-sales tracking

How quickly do builders respond to defects? We track responsiveness across the 2-year liability period.

Regional breakdown

A builder strong in the South East can be poor in the Midlands. We surface performance by region and development.

Value for money

Spec vs. price analysis — what's included as standard versus what other builders charge as extras.

Sustainability ratings

Real-world EPC performance and energy bills — not just developer claims about green credentials.
